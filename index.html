What is LIVE AND DAWLESS?
Welcome to the Live and Dawless official webpage! We are a YouTube program that uploads "dawless" electronic music jams twice a week. The term dawless refers to the act of not using a "DAW" (digital audio workstation). Famous DAWs include Fruity Loops, Pro Tools, Ableton Live, Reaper, Renoise, and many more! The goal of this show is to create music without the assistance of using those computer programs. You will find lot's of work arounds during each performance. From using machines from the late 80's to even using a Nintendo DS (that's not a computer is it?). Sit back and relax while enjoying the latest jams every Wednesday and Saturday! Our main program is availble on YouTube however we have behind the scenes content on our social media websites (Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter).
